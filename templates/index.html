Metric Definitions

(+) Appreciation
Cumulative increase in home market value above purchase price.
(+) Rent Savings
Cumulative rent you avoided by owning instead of renting, growing annually at the rent increase rate.
(-) Mortgage Payments
Cumulative principal + interest paid to the lender over time.
(-) Opportunity Cost
How much your down payment would have grown if invested in the S&P 500 instead, minus the original amount.
(-) Property Tax
Cumulative property taxes paid. Assessed value increases at most 2% per year (Prop 13), capped at market value.
(-) Maintenance
Cumulative home maintenance and repair costs (flat annual amount).
Net Value
Sum of all factors. Positive means buying is ahead; negative means renting would have been better.
